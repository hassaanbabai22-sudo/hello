Orders
How do I place an order?

Browse our Perfume Gallery, click on any product, select your quantity, and tap "Buy Now". Fill in your details and your order will be sent directly to our WhatsApp. We confirm it within 1–2 hours.

How can I track my order?

Every order gets a unique Order ID (e.g. NSC-AB3X7K). Visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID to see the live status of your delivery.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Contact us on WhatsApp as soon as possible. If the order hasn't been dispatched yet, we can make changes. Once shipped, cancellation is not possible.

Delivery
Do you deliver all over Pakistan?

Yes! We deliver nationwide through TCS, Leopards, and M&P. Delivery typically takes 2–5 business days. Quetta orders usually arrive within 1–2 days.

What are the delivery charges?

Delivery charges depend on your city and are calculated at checkout. We occasionally run free delivery promotions — follow us on Facebook or WhatsApp to stay updated.

Products
Are your perfumes 100% original?

Absolutely. Every fragrance in our collection is sourced directly and 100% authentic. We take pride in offering only genuine, original products — no replicas, no imitations.

How long do the perfumes last?

Longevity varies by fragrance type. Our EDP (Eau de Parfum) range typically lasts 6–10 hours, while Oud-based perfumes can last 12+ hours. Check individual product descriptions for details.

Do you offer samples or testers?

Contact us on WhatsApp to ask about availability of testers. We sometimes have sample sets available for customers who want to try before committing to a full bottle.

Payment
What payment methods do you accept?

We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and bank transfer. Payment details are shared after order confirmation on WhatsApp.

Is Cash on Delivery available?

Yes! COD is available for most cities across Pakistan. You pay when your order arrives at your door — simple and secure.

Returns
Can I return or exchange a perfume?

If you receive a damaged, defective, or wrong product, contact us on WhatsApp within 48 hours with your Order ID and photos. We'll arrange an exchange or refund. See our full Returns Policy.

Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?

Yes! We have special pricing for bulk and wholesale orders. Send us a WhatsApp message with your requirements and we'll get you the best deal.

Still have a question?

Our team is on WhatsApp and replies within 1–2 hours.
