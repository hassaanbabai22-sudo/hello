Quick Answers

Do you deliver all over Pakistan?
Yes! We deliver nationwide through TCS, Leopards, and M&P. Delivery typically takes 2–5 business days depending on your city.
Are your perfumes 100% original?
Absolutely. Every fragrance in our collection is sourced directly and 100% authentic. We take pride in offering only genuine products.
Can I return or exchange a perfume?
If you receive a damaged or wrong product, contact us on WhatsApp within 48 hours and we'll arrange an exchange right away.
Do you offer bulk or wholesale pricing?
Yes! We offer special pricing for bulk orders. Send us a WhatsApp message with your requirements and we'll get you the best deal.
How can I track my order?
Visit our Track Order page and enter your Order ID. You can also message us on WhatsApp for a real-time update.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Cash on Delivery (COD), EasyPaisa, JazzCash, and Bank Transfer. Payment details are shared after order confirmation on WhatsApp.
