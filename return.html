Customer Care

Returns & Exchange

Your satisfaction is our priority. If something isn't right with your order, we'll make it right — fast and hassle-free.

How to Return or Exchange

1

Contact Us Within 48 Hours

Message us on WhatsApp within 48 hours of receiving your order with your Order ID and photos of the issue.

2

We Review Your Request

Our team will review your request and confirm eligibility for return or exchange within a few hours.

3

Send Back the Product

We'll arrange pickup or guide you to ship the product back in its original packaging.

4

Replacement Dispatched

Once received and verified, your replacement or refund is processed within 2–3 business days.

Eligible for Return

  • Damaged or broken bottle received
  • Wrong product delivered
  • Leaking or defective packaging
  • Missing items from order

Not Eligible

  • Change of mind after delivery
  • Opened and used perfume
  • Request after 48 hours of delivery
  • Damage caused after delivery

Important: Please take photos or a video when unboxing your order. This helps us process your return request much faster and protects you in case of any delivery damage.

Refunds: Refunds are processed via EasyPaisa, JazzCash, or bank transfer. Cash on Delivery orders will receive a store credit or replacement product.

Need to raise a return request?

Message us on WhatsApp with your Order ID and photos. We'll handle the rest.
