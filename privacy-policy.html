Privacy Policy

At Nexus Scent, we value the data of our fragrance community. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

We collect only the data necessary for order fulfillment and AI scent personalization, such as name, shipping address, and olfactory preferences.

All transaction data is encrypted. We do not store sensitive payment information on our AI fragrance servers.

If you subscribe to our newsletter, your email is used only for Nexus Scent updates and is never shared with third parties.
