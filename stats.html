Pakistan Fragrance Trends 2025

Oud Dominates the Market

Arabian oud-based fragrances account for over 40% of all premium fragrance sales in Pakistan, driven by cultural and religious significance.

+18% YoY Growth

Online Fragrance Sales Rising

E-commerce fragrance purchases in Pakistan have grown 60% since 2022, with WhatsApp-based ordering becoming the most popular channel.

+60% Since 2022

Arabian Brands Outperform Western

Brands like Lattafa, Ahmed Al Maghribi, and Armaf have overtaken many European houses in Pakistani market share due to price-to-quality ratio.

Top Trending

Youth-Driven Demand

18–30 year olds now make up 65% of perfume buyers in Pakistan, preferring bold, long-lasting oriental fragrances with strong projection.

65% of Buyers
