Shipping Policy

At Nexus Scent, we use advanced logistics to ensure your AI-synthesized fragrances reach you in perfect condition across Pakistan.

1. Delivery Timeline

Standard delivery takes 3-5 working days. For major cities like Karachi, Lahore, and Islamabad, we offer expedited 48-hour delivery for AI-selected premium collections.

2. Shipping Charges

We offer Flat Rate shipping of Rs. 250 nationwide. Orders above Rs. 5000 qualify for free robotic-dispatched delivery.

3. Fragrance Safety

Every bottle is sealed in climate-controlled packaging to protect the AI-engineered molecules from heat and light during transit.
